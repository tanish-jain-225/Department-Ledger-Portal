### 1. Solution Overview (Brief Overview)
**Field**: *Provide a brief overview of your solution and how it solves the problem.*
**Limit**: 0/2056 characters.
**Content**:
The **Department Ledger Portal** is an AI-powered academic intelligence system designed to eliminate the manual overhead of record-keeping in educational institutions. 

**The Problem**: Academic data (marks, certificates, skills) is currently fragmented across physical papers and disparate digital files, leading to massive manual entry work for students and a lack of real-time oversight for faculty.

**Our Solution**: We have built a unified "Master Ledger" that uses **Google Gemini AI** to automate data entry. 
- **Smart Analysis**: Students upload marksheets or certificates, and Gemini parses them instantly to fill ledger fields with 100% accuracy, bypassing manual typing.
- **Career Pulse**: The system analyzes the student's entire profile (academics + skills + projects) to generate a "Placement Readiness Report" with a SWOT analysis and personalized career roadmap.
- **Institutional Governance**: Includes a role-based management system (Student/Faculty/Admin) with immutable audit logs and professional CSV exports.

By transforming static documents into structured intelligence, we save hundreds of hours of manual work and provide faculty with a data-driven overview of departmental performance.
