# 🎥 Demo Script - Department Ledger Portal

**Time**: 2–3 Minutes
**Goal**: Show real-world AI document parsing & carrier intelligence in action.

---

## Scene 1: Introduction (15 Seconds)
**Visual**: Dashboard / Landing Page.
**Audio**: 
> "Welcome! This is the Department Ledger Portal—an AI-powered platform designed to turn fragmented academic records into structured institutional intelligence. Built with our **'Obsidian Premium'** design system, the portal is hardened for absolute responsive stability on any device."

---

## Scene 2: Smart Analysis (AI Auto-Fill) (45 Seconds)
**Visual**: Navigate to **Student Records** → **Academic section**. Click **Smart Analysis**.
**Action**: Select a marksheet image (local file). Wait 2 seconds for Gemini to process.
**Audio**: 
> "Normally, entering academic data is manual and error-prone. With our 'Smart Analysis' feature, a student simply uploads a marksheet or certificate. Google Gemini parses the image in the background and instantly fills our form fields—Year, Semester, GPA and even specific subjects—eliminating manual typing entirely!"

---

## Scene 3: Career Pulse (Readiness Report) (45 Seconds)
**Visual**: Navigate to **Career Pulse** → **Generate New Report**.
**Action**: Click the generate button. Wait for the report to appear.
**Audio**: 
> "But we do more than just store data. Our 'Career Pulse' feature uses Gemini to analyze the student's entire profile. Within seconds, it generates a readiness score and a detailed SWOT analysis. It identifies strengths, highlights missing skills and even projects a career roadmap—helping students focus on what matters before final placements."

---

## Scene 4: Institutional Oversight (30 Seconds)
**Visual**: Logout and login as **Admin**. Show the **Governance Hub** and **Audit Stream** modals on a narrowed browser window.
**Action**: Click "Expand" on the Hub and "Inspect" on the Audit logs.
**Audio**: 
> "For faculty and admins, the portal provides total oversight—even on the smallest mobile devices. Our modular 'Governance Hub' and 'Audit Stream' use a **zero-overflow 300px architecture**, providing real-time telemetry and immutable audit logs that ensure unmatched institutional data integrity."

---

## Scene 5: Conclusion (15 Seconds)
**Visual**: Back to Dashboard.
**Audio**: 
> "The Department Ledger Portal is more than just a ledger—it's a foundation for data-driven academic success. No more manual entry, no more data silos—just structured, AI-driven intelligence. Thank you!"

---

## 💡 Quick Tips for the Recording:
1. **Pace**: Speak clearly and don't rush.
2. **Responsive Showcase**: Briefly narrow the browser window to 300px to show the "Obsidian" resilience in action.
3. **Smooth Clicks**: Practice the flow once before recording to keep the cursor movements smooth.
4. **Zoom**: Use browser zoom (Cmd/Ctrl +) if the text looks too small on screen.
5. **Tool**: Use Screen Studio, Loom, or OBS for recording.
